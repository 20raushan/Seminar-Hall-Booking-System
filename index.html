      A Seminar hall is a large room used for instruction, typically at a college or university. Unlike a traditional classroom with a capacity normally between one and fifty, the capacity of seminar halls is typically measured in the hundreds. Lecture theatres almost always have a pitched floor, so that those in the rear are seated higher than those at the front (i.e., tiered seating), allowing them to see the lecturer.
